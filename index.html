Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur is the capital of Malaysia. Its modern panorama is dominated by the Petronas Twin Towers, 451 m high, skyscrapers with glass and steel facades adorned with Islamic motifs. The towers have an observation deck and platform open to the public.

When to get there

As a metropolis, Kuala Lumpur can be visited all year round, but attendance can be particularly important (with many shops and restaurants closed) during the Chinese New Year (Jan./Feb., Variable dates) and towards the end of the year. Ramadan (variable dates). The climate, tropical type, is hot and humid all year round.
